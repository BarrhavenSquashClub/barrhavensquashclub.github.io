Leagues

Barrhaven Squash Club operates 2 leagues, as well as social events, and tournaments. Members can participate in one, or both leagues, as well as tournaments

House League

$25/year

  • Unlimited court access
  • League participation
  • Free clinics

City League

$50/year

  • Valid student ID required
  • All individual benefits

Tournaments

$50/tournament

  • Valid student ID required
  • All individual benefits

*Please note that league fees do not include membership to City of Ottawa Squash facilities.
